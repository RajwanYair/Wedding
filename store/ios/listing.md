## Privacy answers (App Store Connect questionnaire)

1. **Does your app collect data?** Yes
2. **Tracking?** No
3. **Linked to user?** Email address (auth) and phone number (RSVP) when
   the user explicitly signs in or RSVPs.
4. **Third-party SDKs?** Capacitor (no analytics), Supabase (data storage,
   user-initiated), Google Sheets API (user-initiated sync).
5. **Encryption?** Standard HTTPS + AES-GCM at rest in IndexedDB
   (ADR-039). Qualifies for the export-compliance exemption.
